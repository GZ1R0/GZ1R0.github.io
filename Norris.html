Lando Norris

Who is Lando Norris?

Lando Norris, born 13 November 1999, is a British racing driver who competes in Formula One for McLaren. Norris was runner-up in the Formula One World Drivers' Championship in 2024 with McLaren, and has won nine Grands Prix across seven seasons.

Lando is currently 2nd in the 2025 F1 World Driver Championship, with a 25 point gap from 1st (Oscar Piastri) and 44 point lead from 3rd (Max Verstappen).
