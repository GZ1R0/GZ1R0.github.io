Oscar Piastri

Who is Oscar Piastri?

Oscar Jack Piastri, born 6 April 2001, is an Australian racing driver who competes in Formula One for McLaren. Piastri has won nine Formula One Grands Prix across three seasons.

Oscar is currently 1st in the 2025 F1 World Driver Championship, with a 44 point lead from 2nd (Lando Norris) and 69 point lead from 3rd (Max Verstappen).

Oscar's F1 Resume:

  • The first driver to win three consecutive championships in the junior formula series (F4, F3, F2): Oscar
  • First F1 rookie to lead a race in over 10 years: Oscar
  • First rookie on front row since 2010: Oscar
  • First rookie podium since 2017: Oscar
  • Youngest driver to score a grand slam: Oscar
  • Grand Slam: 1x Oscar
  • Fastest lap of all time in Spa-Francochamps circuit for F1: Oscar
  • 2nd Driver with the longest finishing streak in F1: 44x Oscar
  • Highest pole-to-win ratio in F1: (80%: 4 wins / 5 poles) Oscar
  • Fourth driver to complete every single lap: (F1 2024 Season) Oscar

7 Interesting facts about Oscar Piastri:

  1. Oscar currently leads the 2025 F1 World Driver Championship, only being his 3rd season in the sport.
  2. Oscar is known for being nonchalant and relaxed.
  3. His current partner works as a team engineer for the team he races for (McLaren).
  4. He has said that if he weren't an F1 driver, he would an be astronaut one day.
  5. Before racing go-karts, he began his competitive motorport journey in RC cars, winning a national championship at age nine.
  6. Oscar's race engineer used to be an Olympic rower with the British rowing team
  7. The first and only driver in history to win Formula Four, Formula Three, and Formula Two championships in successive seasons.
