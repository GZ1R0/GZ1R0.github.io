Who is Oscar Piastri?

Oscar Jack Piastri, born 6 April 2001, is an Australian racing driver who competes in Formula One for McLaren. Piastri has won nine Formula One Grands Prix across three seasons.

Oscar is currently 1st in the 2025 F1 World Driver Championship, with a 44 point lead from 2nd (Lando Norris) and 69 point lead from 3rd (Max Verstappen).
